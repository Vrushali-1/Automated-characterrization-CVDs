Automated Characterization of Cardiovascular Diseases

The ECG signal contains essential information about the health of heart which can be used for the detection of various CVDs. Nev- ertheless, due to the non-stationary nature of ECG signal, these indicators may appear randomly in the timescale and are tedious to capture visually. Therefore, a novel methodology for automated characterization of CVDs is proposed
